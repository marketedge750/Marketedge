About MarketEdge

Independent, evidence-based investing education — built by one person who got tired of finance content that's either too vague to act on or too salesy to trust.

Why MarketEdge Exists

MarketEdge launched in 2026 with a simple goal: explain investing the way a knowledgeable friend would — clearly, honestly, and without pretending every question has a single right answer. Too much financial content online is either recycled boilerplate or thinly-veiled advertising for a specific broker or product.

We write broker comparisons, portfolio tools, and beginner guides based on publicly available information, our own research, and plain-language explanations of how markets actually work.

Who's Behind This

MarketEdge is an independent publisher, not a registered investment advisory firm, brokerage, or bank. We don't manage money or provide personalized financial advice. Our writers research publicly available data — fees, account minimums, platform features — and present it as clearly as we can.

How We Make Money

MarketEdge is supported by two things: display advertising and, in some cases, affiliate commissions when a reader opens an account through a link on our site, at no extra cost to the reader. Commission relationships never determine our rankings — see our broker comparison methodology for how we evaluate platforms.

Editorial Standards

  • We update pricing and feature data regularly, but always verify current terms directly with a provider before opening an account.
  • We do not accept payment in exchange for a favorable review or ranking.
  • Nothing on this site is personalized financial, tax, or legal advice. See our full disclaimer.
